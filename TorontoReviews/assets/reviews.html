Poke Wrap?!

Welcome back to school, lovely students from across the GTA! You may have noticed by now that Poke Wrap on Yonge street had an amazing deal this past week! $8 for a regular poke wrap or bowl and of course, this includes a stamp on the Poke Wrap loyalty card. Now let's dive into what we thought about this deal and of course, how good is Poke wrap really?

Well, Poke Wrap is definitely on the cheaper side of poke. M'Hito just opened up on Yonge & Edward but its prices have soared through the ceiling. So without any real competitors in the area, is Poke Wrap worth it?
The answer is a resounding YES. Being one of the ONLY healthy food joints on Yonge st, this baby allows you endless customization over your wrap or bowl, which can't be said for most of the other poke joints. In a regular sized option, you're allowed 2 meats, 3 veggies & others, and endless toppings. You can also pick your sauce!
In conclusion, I would recommend this food joint to everyone anyday!

Essential Toronto Madness?!

This is going to be a tough one, essentially because Essential Toronto is the bomb and it is really hard to list the cons of this place. Let's start with the atmosphere. Upon entering this private streetwear shop, you'll see a huge wall of coveted shoes. Some worth more than your cellphone. Additionally, there are racks of expensive, coveted, streetwear clothing taking up most of the square space of the store.

It should be said that the coolest thing this place has to offer besides its items is the yeezy keymaster machine. It's got dozens of Yeezy sneakers and occasionally some Jordans. This machine is the main selling and marketing point of Essential. If you have not seen their Instagram yet, give it a look. They've got hundreds of photos of people winning yeezy's from the keymaster. From what I learned in school, gambling is an ever-growing business, which may be in part why the keymaster at Essential has been so popular. Definitely go in and give the store a browse, you might be surprised what you find/win.
