WORK EXPERIENCE

Katie has work experience in a variety of fields including customer service, food and beverage, as well as digital media and photography.
Note: Pictures are related to (but not direct representations of) companies Katie has worked for. Scroll all the way down to the bottom for all of Katie's recent work experiences!

The Arts and Letters Club of Toronto

The Arts and Letters Club of Toronto is a private club for artists of Toronto. The club hosts a variety of different events each week including dinners, shows, ceremonies, weddings, lunches, performances, and graduations. From May 1st to present, Katie has worked at The Arts and Letters Club of Toronto as a kitchen assistant. Her duties here include food preparation and processing, plate preparation and garnishing, cleaning, and sanitation.

Neshama Therapy

Neshama Therapy is a clinic that specializes in traditional chinese medicine and treatment. Katie started work at Neshama Therapy in April 2016 and currently works at Neshama Therapy as a digital media specialist/content editor. Her duties here include creating business cards/rate cards/digital media for the website, changing the website layout, resolving domain or hosting issues, publishing new changes to the website, and more.

Pride Toronto

Pride Toronto is an organization that hosts the event "Pride" every year. This event is a world wide celebration for inclusiveness. Katie worked for Pride's digital media team as a photographer from April 2016 to July 2016. Her duties consisted of photographing subjects and events, filling out print release forms with subjects, professionally editing all photographs and uploading to Pride's dropbox, and more.

City Sightseeing Toronto

City Sightseeing Toronto is a company that operates city tours around the world on tour buses. Katie worked for City Sightseeing Toronto as a tour guide from September 2016 to November 2016. Her duties here consisted of travelling with groups of passengers on double decker buses, using the microphone to give explanations and history on significant landmarks and attractions, provide an enjoyable and often comedic experience for customers, and upsell/convince guests to buy discounted attraction tickets.

Pusateri's Fine Foods

Pusateri's Fine Foods is the company behind Pusateri's, a luxurious grocery store, catering, and specialty foods chain. Katie worked at Pusateri's Queen street location from November 2016 to January 2017 as a cashier. Her duties here consisted of handling cash and purchases with the POS, providing exceptional customer service, adapting to new product knowledge, upselling customers, and preparing and handling beverages and food.
