Our Program

It took us years to design and blueprint the perfect program to control cravings and promote wellness and it is finally here.

Realization

The first step for a smoker to completely quit smoking is Realization. Realize that there is a problem involved. It's become more than a habit and needs to be stopped. We bring in a team of experts who help the smoker realize they have a problem and make sure that they are willing to fix it.

Cleanse

We provide the smoker with healthy food and emphasize that they focus on other habits they have. Perhaps they have hobbies or they enjoy seeing their family. We will provide them with the opportunity to focus on what they love and have a good time. We will monitor them and prevent them from smoking during this period.

Test

To make sure the smoker is not pretending to be rid of their habit, without their knowledge, we will test them once we feel that they are ready. If they fail this test, we will most likely start the process all over again.

Freedom?

By now, the smoker is not a smoker anymore. We set them free to live their lives and may monitor them for a period of time while they are free. During this time, if they start smoking again, we will pull them back into the program.
