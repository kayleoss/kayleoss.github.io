About Us

NLanding Tech Group was founded in 2017 by Nitsan Green and associates. Our goal is to strive towards creating exceptional experiences and adventures in hospitality using the cutting edge technology of today.

Through generous investments and funding, we have been able to evolve our idea. Diving deeper into our idea, you can see that we are all about improving experiences using the technology and tools that we have been given.

We plan to use our resources to build and create digital experiences for hotels and tourism destinations using technologies like: VR, AR, Machine Learning, and more. Our goal is to use these technologies to create memorable and outright amazing experiences for the consumer.

We believe tourism should be sustainable, and as technology advances, the tourism industry is losing its WOW factor. We're prepared to bring that back. Get started with a free consulting session/meeting with us and we can direct you from there.
