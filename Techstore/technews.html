The Most Beautiful Macbook?

September 5 2017

Could this be the most beautiful Macbook ever made? This 2015 model 12 inch beauty has a 2304 by 1440 resolution screen and packs a punch with a dual core processor at about 1.2ghz each core. With a weight of 2.03 pounds and a width of 28.05, nothing has ever come so close to perfection. Do I want one? Hell YEAH! Can I afford one? Hell NO!

Surface Pro vs Macbook Pro?

September 5 2017

Microsoft seems to be doing a lot of big things lately. I'm not going to lie, when the surface series came out, I considered selling my macbook pro retina on Kijiji to get one. I eventually didn't, because even though that pen would be a lifechanger for me when designing logos and creating art for websites, I just couldn't leave my macbook's amazing trackpad. I mean, not a single trackpad I have ever used has come even close to the macbook pro retina trackpad. But we'll get into that in another post. *winks*

Amazon Echo Uses Alexa

September 5 2017

The Amazon Echo is a great gadget to keep in the home. The other day I wandered upon this little bad boy on Groupon and for the life of me can't remember why I didn't snatch it up. It plays music for you, calls and texts your friends for you, and can even control a bunch of stuff in your house!
