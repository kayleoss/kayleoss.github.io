About Katie

Katie is a motivated, hard-working student at Ryerson University. She has been passionate about technology since she was a child and considers herself a tech enthusiast. Not only is she proficient in modern web technologies, she is continously learning and surpassing her competitors in four main categories:

  • Price
  • Quality
  • Customer Support
  • Aesthetic
Gone are the days of old, laggy, cluttered websites. Katie's goal is to bring her clients a beautiful and minimal finished product. As she continues her studies at Ryerson in business, Katie understands that especially in the digital age, companies only have a few seconds to capture a consumer's attention. Therefore, it is important for websites to be clear, simple, OUTSTANDING, and to the point.
